02 / Research

Protocol research & on-chain intelligence

Our research focuses on protocol design, real usage patterns, and measurable risk. The goal is clarity: what a protocol does, why it works, and where it can break.

Core research areas

AMMs & Liquidity

Pool dynamics, fee models, and liquidity incentives.

Lending Markets

Collateral factors, liquidations, and market health.

Derivatives

Perps design, funding rates, and risk controls.

Restaking & Yield

Yield sources, sustainability, and systemic exposure.

Risk & diligence

DeFi carries technical, economic, and governance risk. Our work emphasizes model transparency and clear communication: assumptions, dependencies, and failure modes.

• Smart contract review checkpoints (design-level)
• Token incentives analysis (reflexivity and dilution)
• On-chain activity signals vs. hype metrics
• Governance and upgradeability exposure
